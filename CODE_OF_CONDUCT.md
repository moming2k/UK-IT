# Code of Conduct

We want this to be a welcoming place for everyone to share ideas and collaborate.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, kind, and constructive.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Credit others' work and contributions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Taking over or claiming another team's work without coordination.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential issue or contact a repository maintainer.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
